Sample Learning Goals

  • Describe the differences between oceanic and continental crust, including their respective properties of density, composition, temperature and thickness.
  • Predict how changes in composition and temperature change crust density and buoyancy.
  • Draw a time series for different types of plate boundaries and their motions, indicating the related surface features.
  • Deduce the type of plate boundary given images or descriptions of surface features.
  • Explain which types of plate boundaries create crust, and which destroy crust
