Title Concept questions for Physics using PhET (Inquiry Based)
Simulations Circuit Construction Kit (DC Only), Forces in 1 Dimension, Faraday's Electromagnetic Lab, Wave on a String, Sound, Geometric Optics, Forces and Motion, Ladybug Motion 2D, Masses & Springs, Projectile Motion, Maze Game, The Ramp, States of Matter, Balloons and Static Electricity, Vector Addition, Gravity and Orbits, Density, Charges and Fields, Energy Skate Park, Pendulum Lab, Fourier: Making Waves, The Moving Man, Ladybug Revolution, Resonance, Ramp: Forces and Motion, Gas Properties, Circuit Construction Kit (AC+DC), Wave Interference, Electric Field Hockey

Description These are the clicker questions that I use in my PhET activities. I update this activity as I add questions. This year, I put them in the order that I use them and included a scope and sequence for my course. I also linked the slides to the activities.
